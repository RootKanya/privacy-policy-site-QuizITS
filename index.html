Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the AI Flashcard App we collects, uses, and discloses your information.

1. Permissions Required

This app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Internet Access: To send your documents to the AI server for processing.
  • Storage (Read/Write): To allow you to select PDF or PPTX files from your device to convert into flashcards.

2. Data Handling

We respect your privacy, Here is how we handle your data:

  • Temporary Processing: Files uploaded (PDFs/PPTX) are sent to the Senopati AI (ElysiaJS/Ollama) endpoint solely for the purpose of generating flashcard questions and answers.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your personal files on our servers after processing is complete.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers directly within the app logic.

3. Third-Party Services

This app utilizes the following third-party technology:

  • Senopati API (Institut Teknologi Sepuluh nopember generative AI model): Used for AI processing.
  • Google Firebase: Used for secure authentication (Login/Logout).
